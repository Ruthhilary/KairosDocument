# Forms & Data Capture

The Forms page is where front-desk data capture happens. It covers altar call responses, baptism and dedication requests, first-time visitor intake, and testimonies.

## Available forms

| Form | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| First-Time Visitor | Welcome a visitor and capture their details for follow-up |
| New Believers Class | Register someone who responded to an altar call into the discipleship programme |
| Baptism | Capture a request to be baptised |
| Testimony | Share a testimony of what God has done |
| Baby Naming | Request a baby naming ceremony |
| Baby Dedication | Request a baby dedication |

Click **Open form →** on any card to open that form.

::: warning Admin and Pastor access only
All six forms are accessible to Admins and Pastors only. Leaders who are part of the Admin department can also review front-desk form submissions. Regular members cannot access the Forms page.
:::

### Filtering submissions

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| Form type | All forms, First-Time Visitor, New Believers Class, Baptism, Testimony, Baby Naming, Baby Dedication |
| Status | All statuses, Pending, Reviewed, Actioned |
| From / To | Date range |

### Exporting submissions

Click **Export CSV** to download all filtered submissions as a spreadsheet. Useful for follow-up workflows outside the system.

## Dormant Attendees

Click **Dormant attendees** to bulk-archive stale form-created contact shells — people who came through a visitor or altar call form but have had no further engagement. This keeps the member directory clean without permanently deleting any records.

## How form submissions connect to other modules

- **New Believers Class** submission → automatically creates an enrolment in the [New Believers](/platform/new-believers) pipeline
- **First-Time Visitor** submission → creates a contact record for follow-up
- **Baptism** submission → creates a request that appears in the admin review queue
- **Baby Naming / Dedication** → creates a request for admin scheduling
