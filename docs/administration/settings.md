# Settings

App Settings let you manage your account security, display preferences, notifications, and privacy.

<div class="screenshot-window screenshot-light">

![App Settings page showing Account and Security, Display, Notifications, and Privacy sections](/screenshots/settings.png)

</div>


Access settings from your **Profile** page — click **Settings** in the top-right corner, or use the gear icon at the bottom of the sidebar.

## Account & Security

### Change password

Update your login password. You'll need to provide your current password before setting a new one.

### Change email

Move your account to a new email address. Both the old and new address receive a notification when the change is made.

### Recent sign-ins

View a history of sign-ins, password changes, and role updates on your account. Useful for spotting unauthorised access.

## Display

### Theme

Choose between **Light**, **Dark**, or match your device's system preference. The dark mode toggle is also available directly from the bottom of the sidebar.

### Language

Currently English only. Additional languages are coming in a future release.

## Notifications

### Email preferences

Manage which email categories reach your inbox and how often. Choose the types of notifications you want to receive — announcements, reminders, alerts, and more.

## Privacy & Data

### Legal & consent

Review the Terms of Service, Privacy Notice, and manage your marketing preferences.

### Export my data

Download a copy of your personal data (GDPR right to portability). **Coming soon.**

### Delete my account

Withdraw consent and request account deletion. **Coming soon.**

---

## Log Out

The **Log Out** button appears at the bottom of the Settings page. Clicking it ends your session immediately and returns you to the login screen.
