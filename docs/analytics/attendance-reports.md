# Attendance Reports

Attendance reports give you a clear view of service and fellowship participation trends over time — helping you spot drops early and respond before they become patterns.

## Accessing attendance data

Go to **Reports** and the page opens on the **Attendance** tab by default.

## Service attendance

### Weekly Attendance Rate chart

A line chart showing the average attendance rate (percentage of active members present) over the last 8 weeks. Each data point represents one week.

- A flat line near 80%+ is healthy
- A consistent downward slope needs attention
- A sharp drop on a specific date may indicate a special event or service cancellation

### Trend indicator

The chart header shows a trend direction badge:
- **Trending up** — attendance improving
- **Trending down** — attendance declining (shown in red as a warning)

### Monthly Highlight

A plain-language summary on the right side of the chart. For example:

> "Attendance needs attention — Average rate is 80% over the last 8 weeks"

Click **View breakdown** to drill into the detail.

## Fellowship attendance

### Top Fellowships Attended

A bar chart ranking fellowships by total meeting attendance. The bar length represents the number of members who attended meetings in the period. Click any bar to see that fellowship's full meeting history.

This helps you identify which fellowships are thriving and which might need pastoral support or schedule adjustments.

## My Attendance (individual view)

Every member has a personal attendance report on their **My Attendance** page:

- 12-week attendance rate
- Punctuality breakdown (Present, Late, Virtual, Missed) as a bar chart
- Current attendance streak (consecutive services attended)
- Full service history for the window, oldest first

This is private — only the member, their pastor, and admins can see it.

## Service types in reports

Attendance data includes all service types:
- Sunday Service
- Midweek Service
- Special Service
- Prayer Meeting

Filter by service type when you want to isolate Sunday attendance vs. midweek engagement.

## Exporting attendance data

Use **Export CSV** on the Attendance page to download a full attendance dataset for the current filter. Date range and branch filters apply to the export.

## Role-based scope

| Role | What they see |
|------|--------------|
| Admin | All branches, all services |
| Pastor | Their branch only |
| Leader | Their fellowship or department only |
| Member | Own attendance only (My Attendance page) |
