# Attendance Tracking

Kairos tracks two types of attendance: **service attendance** for Sunday, midweek, and special services, and **fellowship meeting attendance** for small group gatherings.

## Service attendance

Service attendance is managed from the **Attendance** page in the sidebar.

### Who records it

Admins, Pastors, and Leaders with branch permissions can record service attendance.

### Creating a service record

1. Go to **Attendance**
2. Click **+ Record a service**
3. Fill in branch, service type, date, and optional details (theme, preacher, topic)
4. Save — the service appears in the list

### Checking members in

1. Find the service in the list
2. Click **Check In →**
3. Mark each member as **Present**, **Absent**, or **Virtual**
4. Optionally flag a member as a **First-Time Visitor**
5. Save the roll

### Service types

| Type | Use case |
|------|----------|
| Sunday Service | Regular weekly service |
| Midweek Service | Wednesday or other midweek |
| Special Service | Seasonal or one-off events |
| Prayer Meeting | Dedicated prayer gatherings |
| Other | Any other format |

## Fellowship meeting attendance

Fellowship attendance is managed from inside a fellowship's detail page, not from the main Attendance page.

### Who records it

Fellowship leaders and co-leaders record attendance for their own fellowship. Pastors and Admins can record for any fellowship in their branch.

### Creating a meeting record

1. Go to **Fellowships** and open a fellowship
2. Navigate to the meetings tab
3. Click **+ New Meeting**
4. Set the date, topic, location, and optional notes
5. Save

### Taking attendance

1. Open an existing meeting
2. Mark each member as **Present**, **Absent**, **Excused**, or **Late**
3. Add per-member notes if needed
4. Save

## My Attendance (personal view)

Every member has a private **My Attendance** page in the sidebar. It shows:

- 12-week attendance rate with a trend comment
- Services attended vs total services in the window
- Last seen date and service
- Punctuality breakdown (Present, Late, Virtual, Missed)
- Current attendance streak
- Full service history for the 12-week window

This page is visible only to the member, their pastor, and admins.

---

## Attendance in reports

Service attendance trends appear in **Reports → Attendance tab** for Pastors and Admins. The chart shows average attendance rate over the last 8 weeks per branch, with trend direction indicators.

Leaders see attendance data scoped to their own fellowship or department members only.
