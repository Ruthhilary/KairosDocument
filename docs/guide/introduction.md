# Introduction

Kairos is a church administration system built for managing branches, members, fellowships, departments, outreach programs, and more. It's designed as a modern operations console — fast to scan, role-aware, and built for real workflows.

## What it covers

- **Branches** — Multiple church locations, each with its own leadership, members, and data.
- **Members** — Full member profiles, roles, soft deletes, and branch scoping.
- **Fellowships** — Small groups with leaders, meeting schedules, and attendance tracking.
- **Departments** — Ministry departments (Choir, Ushers, etc.) per branch, with rotas and uniforms.
- **Outreach** — Event-based evangelism with a souls pipeline, follow-ups, and conversion tracking.
- **New Believers** — Dedicated pipeline for recently converted members.
- **Reports & Analytics** — Attendance trends, outreach stats, and department activity.
- **Auth** — JWT-based authentication with approval workflow and functional grants.

## Who this is for

- **Developers** building or extending the system.
- **Admins** deploying and configuring a Kairos instance.
- **Branch leaders** and department heads using the system day-to-day.
